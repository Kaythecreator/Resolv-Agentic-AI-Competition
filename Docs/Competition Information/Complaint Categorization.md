**Financial Services**

**Complaint Categorization:** A national fintech company offering credit cards, loans, and digital banking services is facing a surge in customer complaints from multiple channels, including the CFPB Consumer Complaint Database ([https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/](https://www.consumerfinance.gov/data-research/consumer-complaints/)). Complaints are inconsistently categorized, manually routed, and often delayed, increasing regulatory risk and customer churn. The business challenge is to build an agentic AI system that automatically classifies complaints (by product, issue type, severity, and compliance risk), identifies root causes, assigns the appropriate internal teams, and generates a complete resolution plan—including remediation steps, regulatory-compliant customer responses, and preventive recommendations. The system must coordinate specialized agents, provide explainable decisions suitable for regulators, scale to large volumes of unstructured complaint narratives, and include measurable performance metrics for accuracy, resolution quality, fairness, and customer satisfaction impact.
